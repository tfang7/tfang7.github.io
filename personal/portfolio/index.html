Cyclops and Rats

  1. Unity3D Game

    I designed the level, created models using basic Unity 3D shapes, textures using Substance Designer, and programmed the AI and lights.

  2. Theme

    The theme of this game was constrained to the keyword "ascend".

  3. Gameplay

    You play the role of a miner who is trapped in an elevator with a mining machine that has malfunctioned. The elevator is slowly running out of energy. Conveniently there are energy producing bugs skittering around the elevator. Your goal is to trap the bugs with your net gun while evading the machine and bring the bugs to the pylon in the center that powers the shaft.

Mech Racer (Working Title)

  1. Unreal Engine 4 Game

    Work In Progress (5 weeks in). Currently programming vehicle movement and race mechanics.

  2. Theme

    This is an epic humanoid mech racing game. It is based in a future and will involve a skill-based racing style and include plenty of mech combat!

  3. Gameplay

    We are creating this game in order to create a more skill-based racing game. The Mario Kart series is a prime example of a successful racing game, however it is a game geared towards groups of families and friends. We want to create a game that requires deep understanding of the mechanics in order to encourage competitive play.
